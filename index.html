"I WAS ONCE A GREAT CONQUEROR. MY HANDS BORE A ROARING WEAPON THAT SWEPT NATIONS INTO THE SEAS. MY ARMIES WERE MASTERFUL PAINTERS- WHAT FOLLOWED THEM WAS A BRUSHSTROKE OF BLOOD AND ASH THAT STRETCHED TO EVERY HORIZON. BUT MY SWORD ARM HAS GROWN TIRED, AND MY RESOLVE HAS GROWN WEARY. SOMEWHERE UNDER HEAVEN IS A MASTER OF VOICES. THEY CAN ASK FLOWERS TO GROW FROM THE DRIEST DESERT. THEY CAN SING A SONG SO BEAUTIFUL THAT IT HALTS ARMIES IN THEIR MARCH. I WISH TO FIND THIS PERSON. I WISH TO BE INSPIRED BY THEM. I WISH TO BE CONVINCED THAT SWORDSMEN LIKE ME NEED NOT EXIST."
